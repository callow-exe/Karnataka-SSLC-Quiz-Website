Service Overview

Karnataka SSLC Quiz – Android application is a quiz app, which is made for (but not limited to) Karnataka SSLC students. The app currently supports Maths, Science, and Social subjects in both Kannada and English medium. The app is completely accessible only in the availability of network. Each Quiz has 5 questions which are randomly selected from a number of questions in the database, which is frequently updated. The app also gives a feature for two users to play the quiz simultaneously (only if one of the users has the phone number of the other saved in the device), and the winner is displayed at the end of the quiz.

You can use the app without signing in, however all features will only be available if you sign in.
