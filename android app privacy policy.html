Karnataka SSLC Quiz - Android application - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand how the information provided by you is managed, when you are using Karnataka SSLC Quiz - Android application. Your trust in providing your information is of great value, and necessary actions will be taken to protect and put it to proper use.

Information Collected Directly

  1. If you choose to Sign in to Karnataka SSLC Quiz - Android application your Phone number will have to be provided. This phone number will be collected and used to connect you with other users who are having your phone number and are using the app.
  2. If you choose to Sign in to Karnataka SSLC Quiz - Android application You may give your Name (not mandatory, even if you are signed in), which may be used in further versions of the application. You can remove the name whenever you want to, and will have no traces.

Note: You may be asked for permission to access your contacts, your contacts information is not collected, i.e., not stored in the database, and will only be used when the app is processing to show which of your contacts are using the app. You can disable the permission in the settings, and the service related to it will stop working immediately.

You can Delete your account in the account section of the app. Ensure proper internet connection while deleting your account as it may leave traces. After you delete your account all the data collected directly by the app (does not include Google analytics data), will be erased permanently. If any traces are left, the data will be deleted as soon as possible.
